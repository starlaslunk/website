Portrait of Rachel Curry

I did this sketch of my friend Rachel way back in 2013. I usually hate my attempts at portraits from life, but I've always had a soft spot for this one and I feel it's a pretty good likeness. You don't know what Rachel looks like (unless you know her, obviously) so I guess you'll just have to take my word for it.
