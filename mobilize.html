Illustrations for Mobilize Podcast

Every once in a while I'm asked to do an illustration for the Mobilize Podcast, which you can listen to here if you are interested. Like a lot of other efforts, the podcast began in reaction to the election of Donald Trump. Though my own politics are almost invariably to the left (whatever the hell that means anymore) of the content of the podcast, the illustrations are a lot of fun to do.
