Potrait of Tula

I did a portrait of Tula, the dog the Tea Room at the Museum of Jurassic Technology is named after as a gift. I was reading Rabelais' "Gargantua and Pantagruel" at that time, so there's a quote from it. Georgian (the country) themes abound. I'm a little disappointed that I didn't get a better scan of it when I could.
